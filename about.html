Natural Language Processing (NLP)

At the heart of NeuRoDetect is state-of-the-art NLP technology. We use deep learning models based on the Transformer architecture to understand human language just like a linguistic expert would, but at machine speed.

1. Lexical Analysis

Fake news often relies on specific emotional triggers, hyperbolic adjectives, and urgent language. Our models scan for deviations from standard journalistic integrity markers.

2. Semantic Understanding

We don't just match keywords; we analyze the relationships between entities. If a claim logically contradicts known facts or violates fundamental physics of the story, our certainty score adjusts accordingly.

3. Stylometric Features

Every publication has a fingerprint. Bots, click-farms, and propagandists leave numerical traces in their syntax, capitalization rates, and punctuation usage which our AI can instantly flag.
